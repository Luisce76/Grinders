Rating Guide

GREAT

  • Time: 45 minutes per bale
  • Produces SH/SP
  • Easy to grind
  • Comes very clean

Moderate

  • Time: 1 hour to 1 hour 20 minutes
  • Produces SH/SP, lightly contaminated, FL, or flakesorter
  • Some difficulty grinding or hard to pull apart
  • Some light contamination

Bad

  • Time: over 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Produces FL(.30) or heavy contamination
  • Difficult to grind or very compressed
  • Heavily contaminated or hard to clean out
